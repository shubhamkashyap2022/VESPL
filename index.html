About Us

Vitasta Enviro Services Pvt. Ltd. (VESPL) is promoted by young Environmental Engineering Graduates and was incorporated as a private limited company in January 2018. VESPL is a quality, health, safety, environmental and business management consulting company focused on ensuring satisfaction of client and compliance of legislative requirements and standards whilst empowering them with the knowledge, systems and processes to self-manage into the future. VESPL offers following Environmental MSTC E-Auction Registration for Industrial, Mining & Infrastructure projects:-

  • EIA Study for obtaining EC
  • CTE/CTO, CGWA Permission & Compliances
  • Environmental Monitoring
  • Mine Planning & Surveying
  • ETP/STP/WWTP/CETP/RWH

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSULTANCY SERVICES

MSTC E-Auction Registration

Registration of applicant/lessee on web portal of MSTC_ E-Auction.

Land Conversion for Mining

Providing Technical assistance in conversion of Pvt. Lands to Mining Area.

Mining Plan & Pre-Feasibility Report

Preparation of Mining Plan/Scheme of Mining, Remote Sensing DGPS Survey in pursuance of IBM Circular dated 6th April 2010 & preparation of Pre-Feasibility Report (PFR) as per UNFC Guidelines & IBM Circular.

DGMS Permissions

Providing consultation services for obtaining necessary permission/clearance from DGMS for Blasting & Heavy Earth Moving Machineries (HEMMs).

Pollution Control Feasibility

Preparation of Feasibility Reports regarding prevention and control of pollution.

Environmental Clearance (EC)

Preparation & submission of application for obtaining Environmental Clearance (EC) from DEIAA/SEIAA/MoEFCC as per EIA Notification dated 14/09/2006 and its amendments.

EC & Consent Compliance

Preparation & submission of Compliance Reports of Environmental Clearances (EC)/Conditions of consents (CTE/CTO) under Water Act, 1974 / Air Act, 1981 & Authorization under HWM Rules.

CTE/CTO & HWM Authorization

Providing assistance in preparation & submission of online applications for obtaining Consent to Establish (CTE) / Consent to Operate (CTO) under Water Act, 1974 / Air Act, 1981 / Authorization under HWM Rules.

Environmental Sampling

Collection & Sampling of Water / Air/ Soil/ Noise samples by approved Laboratory.

CGWA NOC for Groundwater

Preparation & submission of application along with required technical backup for obtaining NOC / permission from CGWA for ground water extraction.

Forest Clearance

Forest Clearance Consultancy from the Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, New Delhi as per Forest (Conservation) Act, 1980.

Biodiversity & Conservation Plans

Forest Conservation Plan, Wildlife Conservation Plan, Ecology and Biodiversity Study.

Social Impact Assessment (SIA)

Social Impact Assessment for major projects involving Resettlement & Rehabilitation, Resettlement Action Plan confirming to multilateral body guidelines.

Risk & Disaster Management

Risk Assessment, Disaster Management Plan/Damage Control Study.

Rainwater Harvesting Systems

Design, fabrication, supply, installation and commissioning of Rain Water Harvesting Systems and providing Technical Assistance in matters related with CGWA Permissions and Annual Compliances.

Air Pollution Control Systems

Design, fabrication, supply, installation and commissioning of Air Pollution Control systems for all type of industrial applications.

ETP/STP Installation

Design, fabrication, supply, installation and commissioning of Effluent Treatment Plants (ETP’s)/Sewage Treatment Plant (STP’s) based on economical and proven technologies suitable for individual industrial/infrastructural unit.

ETP/STP Upgradation

Technical & capacity up-gradation of Effluent Treatment Plants/Sewage Treatment Plant (STP’s).

Environment Audit & Statement

Environment Auditing & preparation of Environmental Statement reports.
